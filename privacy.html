Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Plain-language summary.

Nschess stores progress and preferences on your device. If you create an online Player Pass, Nschess also stores the account and game information needed to provide sign-in, friends, challenges, tournaments, and leaderboards. Nschess does not sell personal information or use it for targeted advertising.

Information Nschess handles

Depending on the features you use, this can include your email address for authentication, username, profile choices, country flag, chess ratings, game results and moves, puzzle and lesson progress, friend connections, private challenge details, chat messages, tournament participation, device-stored preferences, and account-security session data.

Why Nschess uses it

Nschess uses this information to sign you in, preserve progress, personalize lessons and bots, run online chess features, display leaderboards you choose to join, prevent abuse, respond to support requests, and keep the service secure.

Where information is stored

Local progress and settings are saved in your browser storage until you clear them or remove local data. Online account features use Supabase for authentication and database services. The web app may load the Supabase client from a content delivery network and may request public chess-puzzle data. These providers process only the information needed to provide their part of the service.

Sharing and visibility

Profile, rating, and match-history visibility follow the settings you choose where the feature supports it. Other players can see information that is intentionally shared through leaderboards, friends, challenges, tournaments, or chat. Nschess does not sell personal information.

Retention and deletion

Device-only data remains on your device until cleared. Online account data is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion from the in-app Privacy settings or the Account Deletion page. Deleting an online account removes the account and associated cloud records supported by the service; limited records may be retained only when required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

Your choices

You can adjust visibility, friend requests, online status, match-history visibility, notifications, and stored local profile details in Settings. You can also contact us to ask about your data or request deletion.

Security and contact

Nschess uses HTTPS for hosted connections and access controls for online account data. No internet service can guarantee absolute security. For privacy questions or deletion assistance, contact hello@checkmatequest.app.
