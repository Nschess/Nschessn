Account controls

Delete your Nschess account

This action is permanent.

If you can sign in, use Settings → Privacy → Delete online account for the fastest deletion. It removes your cloud account and associated Nschess data supported by the service, then clears Nschess data stored in that browser.

Request deletion without signing in

If you cannot access the app, email us from the address associated with your Player Pass. Include your Nschess username and the phrase “Delete my Nschess account.” We may ask for enough information to verify the request before deleting data.

Request account deletion

What is deleted

Deletion removes the online account and its associated cloud profile, progress, friend connections, challenges, messages, tournament participation, and leaderboard entry where those features are enabled. Device-only progress can also be removed from the in-app deletion flow. Limited information may be retained only when required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.
